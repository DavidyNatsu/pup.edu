About PUP Mulanay


PUP Mulanay serves as “The Avenue of Knowledge in the Countryside”.

The campus supports PUP's mission to democratize access to quality education to poor but deserving students of Bondoc Peninsula area of Quezon Province and the nearby provinces. It is located at Brgy. Sta. Rosa, Mulanay, Quezon, along the Bondoc Peninsula Hi-way, overlooking the Tayabas Bay and the island of Marinduque.
